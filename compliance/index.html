Credentials & Certifications

Hephzibah King's Mother is committed to maintaining the highest standards of compliance and professional excellence. As a certified minority-owned business, we pride ourselves on transparency and adherence to regulatory requirements.

FDLE/FBI Approved LiveScan Provider

We are an approved provider for the Florida Department of Law Enforcement (FDLE) and Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) for LiveScan fingerprint data collection services. This certification allows us to conduct Level 2 background screenings for positions of trust involving children and vulnerable adults.

Minority-Owned Business Certification

Our firm is certified as a minority-owned business, demonstrating our commitment to diversity and inclusion in the business community.
