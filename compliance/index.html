Level 2 Background Screening

Level 2 background screenings are the most comprehensive background checks available in Florida. They are required by state law for individuals working in positions of trust with vulnerable populations.

What is Included:

  • State and national criminal history checks through FDLE and FBI databases
  • Sex offender registry search
  • Fingerprint-based identification verification

Who Needs Level 2 Screening:

  • Childcare workers and educators
  • Healthcare professionals working with vulnerable adults
  • Home health aides and caregivers
  • Volunteers and staff at facilities serving children or vulnerable populations
  • Other positions mandated by Florida law
