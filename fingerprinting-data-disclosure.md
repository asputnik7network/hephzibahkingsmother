# Fingerprinting & Data Handling

Last updated: 2026-01-15

## Overview

Hephzibah King's Mother is an approved Florida Department of Law Enforcement (FDLE) and Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) LiveScan provider. We collect digital fingerprints for Level 2 background screenings in accordance with strict regulatory standards and best practices for data security and privacy.

## What is Level 2 Background Screening?

A Level 2 background screening is a comprehensive state and national criminal history check that uses fingerprints to search:
- Florida criminal records (through FDLE)
- National criminal records (through FBI)
- National and state sex offender registries

Level 2 screenings are required by Florida law for individuals working in positions of trust with:
- Children
- Vulnerable adults (elderly or disabled individuals)
- Other sensitive or high-trust roles

## Fingerprint Data Collection Process

### 1. Appointment Scheduling
You schedule an appointment through our website (http://calendly.com/DOESS) or by contacting us directly.

### 2. Identification and Authorization
At your appointment, you must provide:
- Valid government-issued photo identification
- Authorization from your employer or requesting organization
- Any required forms or documentation

### 3. Digital Fingerprint Capture
We use LiveScan technology to:
- Capture high-quality digital fingerprint images
- Verify the quality and completeness of fingerprint data
- Ensure accurate transmission to FDLE and FBI databases

### 4. Secure Transmission
Fingerprint data is transmitted electronically through secure, encrypted channels directly to:
- Florida Department of Law Enforcement (FDLE)
- Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI)

### 5. Processing and Results
- FDLE and FBI process the fingerprints and conduct the background check
- Results are typically available within 24-72 hours
- Results are sent to the requesting organization (employer, licensing agency, etc.)
- We do not receive or review the results of background checks

## Data Privacy and Compliance

### Regulatory Compliance
We comply with all applicable regulations, including:
- Florida Statutes Chapter 943 (Florida Criminal Justice Information)
- FBI Criminal Justice Information Services (CJIS) Security Policy
- State and federal privacy laws
- FDLE LiveScan provider requirements

### Data Usage
Your fingerprint data is used exclusively for:
- Level 2 background screening purposes
- Transmission to authorized law enforcement databases (FDLE, FBI)
- Compliance with legal requirements

We do NOT:
- Sell or share fingerprint data with third parties for marketing
- Retain fingerprint images longer than required by law
- Use fingerprint data for any purpose other than background screening

### Data Retention
- Fingerprint images are transmitted to FDLE/FBI and are not retained locally except as required by regulation
- Personal information (name, contact details) is retained as required for compliance and record-keeping
- All retention periods comply with FDLE and FBI requirements

### Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Understand how your fingerprint data is used
- Receive information about the background check process
- Challenge the accuracy of criminal history records (through FDLE/FBI, not through our office)
- File complaints regarding privacy violations

## Third-Party Involvement

### FDLE and FBI
Your fingerprint data is transmitted to:
- **Florida Department of Law Enforcement (FDLE)**: Processes state criminal history checks
- **Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI)**: Processes national criminal history checks

Both agencies maintain their own privacy policies and data handling procedures.

### Requesting Organizations
The results of your background screening are provided to the organization that requested it (e.g., employer, licensing board). We do not control how requesting organizations use or store background check results.
