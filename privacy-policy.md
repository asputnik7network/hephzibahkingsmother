# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 15, 2026

Hephzibah King's Mother ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:
- Schedule an appointment or request services
- Contact us via email or contact forms
- Submit inquiries or feedback

This information may include:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Mailing address
- Other contact or identifying information you choose to provide

### Biometric Information
**Important:** No biometric or fingerprint data is collected through this website. Biometric data (fingerprints) is collected only in person at our authorized location using secure, government-approved LiveScan systems in compliance with FDLE and FBI requirements.

When you visit our location for fingerprinting services:
- Fingerprints are captured electronically using LiveScan equipment
- Data is transmitted directly to FDLE/FBI systems
- We do not store fingerprint images or biometric data on our systems
- All biometric data handling follows Florida Statutes and federal regulations

### Automatic Information
When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device, including:
- IP address
- Browser type
- Operating system
- Referring URLs
- Pages viewed
- Date and time of visits

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve our services
- Process appointments and service requests
- Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
- Send administrative information, updates, and service-related notices
- Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
- Detect, prevent, and address technical issues or fraudulent activity

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

### Service Providers
We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, provided they agree to keep information confidential.

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., court orders, subpoenas, or government agencies).

### Background Screening
For fingerprinting services, fingerprint data is transmitted directly to:
- Florida Department of Law Enforcement (FDLE)
- Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI)
- Authorized requesting agencies as specified in your screening request

This transmission is required for Level 2 background screenings and follows established legal protocols.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

For biometric data collected in person:
- LiveScan equipment is maintained according to FDLE/FBI standards
- Data transmission is encrypted
- No local storage of fingerprint images occurs
- All systems comply with Criminal Justice Information Services (CJIS) security requirements

## Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected and to comply with legal, regulatory, or business requirements.

For fingerprinting services:
- We retain appointment and administrative records as required by law
- Fingerprint images are not retained by our organization
- Background check results are maintained by FDLE/FBI according to their retention policies

You have the right to:
