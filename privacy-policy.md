# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 15, 2026

## Introduction

Hephzibah King's Mother ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:
- Contact us through our website forms
- Schedule appointments
- Request our services
- Subscribe to our communications

This may include:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Mailing address
- Service-related information

### Biometric Data
**Important Notice:** No biometric or fingerprint data is collected through this website. Biometric data, including fingerprints, is collected **only in person** using authorized FDLE/FBI LiveScan systems in compliance with all applicable federal and state regulations.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, operate, and maintain our services
- Communicate with you about appointments and services
- Process your requests and inquiries
- Send you administrative information
- Improve our website and services
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

### Fingerprint Data Security
All fingerprint and biometric data is:
- Collected only in person using authorized systems
- Transmitted directly to FDLE/FBI databases
- Not stored on our local systems
- Handled in compliance with federal and state regulations

## Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:
- Service providers who assist in our operations
- Law enforcement or regulatory agencies when required by law
- FDLE/FBI for background screening purposes (fingerprint data only)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements)
- Opt-out of marketing communications

## Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (such as scheduling platforms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

## Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last Updated" date and will be effective upon posting.
