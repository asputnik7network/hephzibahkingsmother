Fingerprint Data Collection

As a Florida Department of Law Enforcement (FDLE) and Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) approved LiveScan provider, we collect digital fingerprints for Level 2 background screenings. A Level 2 screening is a comprehensive state and national check that uses fingerprints to search criminal records and the sex offender registry via the FDLE and FBI databases. These screenings are required for positions of trust that involve working with children or vulnerable adults.

Our streamlined process ensures accuracy and confidentiality. Contact us to schedule your appointment and experience professional, courteous service.
