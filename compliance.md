## Fingerprinting and Background Screening Compliance

### FDLE Approval
As an approved Florida Department of Law Enforcement (FDLE) LiveScan provider, we:
- Maintain current FDLE authorization and good standing
- Comply with all FDLE requirements for fingerprint data collection
- Follow FDLE protocols for transmission and quality assurance
- Undergo regular audits and compliance reviews by FDLE

### FBI Authorization
As an FBI-approved LiveScan provider, we:
- Comply with FBI Criminal Justice Information Services (CJIS) Security Policy
- Maintain required security measures for biometric data handling
- Follow FBI protocols for national background check processing
- Participate in FBI compliance and quality assurance programs

### Florida Statutes Compliance
We adhere to relevant Florida laws, including:
- **Chapter 943, Florida Statutes**: Criminal Justice Information
- **Chapter 435, Florida Statutes**: Employment Screening
- **Chapter 110, Florida Statutes**: State Employment
- Other applicable state regulations regarding background screening
