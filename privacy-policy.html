Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-15

Introduction

Hephzibah King's Mother ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services, including our fingerprint data collection services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:
- Schedule an appointment for fingerprinting services
- Contact us through our website or email
- Submit forms or requests for services
- Engage our coaching or consulting services

This may include:
- Name and contact information (email, phone number, address)
- Identification information required for background screening services
- Biometric data (fingerprints) for Level 2 background screening
- Payment information for services rendered

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device, including:
- IP address
- Browser type
- Operating system
- Referring URLs
- Pages viewed and time spent on pages

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, operate, and maintain our services
- Process fingerprint data collection and Level 2 background screenings
- Communicate with you about services, appointments, and inquiries
- Send you administrative information and service updates
- Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
- Improve our website and services
- Prevent fraud and enhance security

Fingerprint Data and Background Screening

As an FDLE/FBI approved LiveScan provider, we handle fingerprint data in accordance with:
- Florida Department of Law Enforcement (FDLE) regulations
- Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) requirements
- All applicable state and federal privacy laws

Fingerprint data is:
- Collected digitally and transmitted securely to FDLE and FBI databases
- Used solely for the purpose of conducting Level 2 background screenings
- Retained only as required by law and regulatory requirements
- Protected with appropriate technical and organizational security measures

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information:
- With FDLE and FBI for background screening purposes
- With authorized employers or organizations requesting background checks (with your consent)
- With service providers who assist in our operations (under confidentiality agreements)
- When required by law, court order, or legal process
- To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Fingerprint data is retained in accordance with FDLE and FBI requirements.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:
- The right to access your personal information
- The right to correct inaccurate information
- The right to request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)
- The right to object to or restrict certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate parental consent.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
